VOTNEG Ministerial Arms

VOTNEG ARMS

VOTNEG understands the rate of diversity in the world we live in today. For this purpose, the ministry has created diverse areas through which her members can learn how to realize and maximize their respective potentials deposit in them during creation. The following paragraphs below clearly outline details about the various arms available in VOTNEG.

VOTNEG VOICE

VOTNEG VOICE is an arm that exists to activate and sustain the manifest presence of God in an atmosphere of intense worship and praise that touches the very heart of God while bringing healing to the souls of men.
It is our believe that the primary purpose of humanity is to worship God Almighty. Since Music is one of the main ways to venerate and extol God, VOTNEG VOICE therefore uses it as a platform to lead and engage people in moments of authentic heart to heart worship and praise.
As an arm in the Ministry, it seeks to make a perfect blend between songs inspired by the Holy Ghost and the melodious voices of different ministers in order to primarily release echoes to the heavens and also captivate its audience thus bringing them to a state of total submission to God.

Our ministrations have always been life touching; discharging an atmosphere of healing, miracles and most especially translating lost souls from darkness to the Kingdom of light.
Members of this arm have been sharpened by adequate teachings, trainings in the word thus causing everyone to grow spiritually and birth forth songs from the undiluted word of God that is able to bring transformation.

Over the years, this arm has hosted multiple events in different platforms (retreats, crusades, concerts and conferences etc.) with one of its most successful event tagged “Praise and Worship festival” where ministers and believers from all over the nation gathered to raise exquisite worship to God.
We look forward to greater events and impact.

2. VOTNEG SUPER MODEL DANCE CREW

VSMD (VOTNEG Super Model Dance Crew) was initiated to minister to the hearts of people and win souls into the kingdom of God through spirit-led dance moves.
VSMD believes God is also revealed through dance in a unique way and it’s committed to showcasing his infinite love, mercy, grace and goodness through it to the world. It identifies, develops and promotes people who are gifted and also trains individuals who are passionate but have no skills with diverse dancing abilities.
One of its goals is to make dancing more than just an entertainment but an act of worship releasing a message to its audience about the wonders of God.
In major events within the ministry, this arm has intrigued minds and left audiences in good cheer within the confines of the scriptures.
VOTNEG’s annual conferences, weekly meetings and other events have often been graced by their flamboyant ministrations.

VOTNEG MOVIE HOUSE

VMH has as its main aim to display the intent of God for humanity through skits with a blend of comedy. With various timely presentations which spans from a believer’s day to day walk with God, interpretation and application of scriptures, decisions and destiny, VMH longs to impress an incessant desire in the hearts of its audience to align to God’s plans.
It also seeks to replicate major biblical stories in the simplest possible way so as to promote knowledge about biblical truths. VMH is not just limited to acting out biblical stories but it also dramatizes inspired stories that are relevant and applicable to contemporary times.
One of the greatest strengths of this arm is that it uses these skits as an effective tool for soul winning. Over the years, it has picked out, nurtured and exposed people with immense passion for drama, humor, and poetry amongst many others thus inculcating in them the fire to capture any audience by the excellency of talents and anointing.
This arm like any other has many achievements with the most prominent being the ‘Laugh Therapy’ which is a comedy series designed to cure stress and put a smile on the faces of its spectators. Despite these achievements, it still forges ahead for greater accomplishments.

VKMP

VKMP is out to showcase the rare beauty of God in the most descent way through exquisite parades and to proof to the world that real beauty and its perfection comes from Him only. We are redefining beauty and all it entails in the light of God's word.
We also believe that our bodies, irrespective of size, height and color were made for worship. Therefore, we see a generation of models who are the very reflection of God, dignifying him with their bodies so that all men may see and be drawn for impact and transformation.

Every VKMP model believes that there is more to modeling than simply walking back-and-forth on a runway. This arm doesn’t see parade and modeling as a mere show of beauty, clothes and cat walking techniques. Rather, it perceives it as an avenue to express a godly frame of mind, consciousness and perspective.
Also, the arm carries an unquenchable message of Christ as it parades and injects a ‘vaccine’ into the hearts of individuals to know God more. That’s a result worth celebrating!

LOFAEX

LOFAEX is a special arm of VOTNEG involved in the gathering, assessing, creating, and presenting of news and information.
It sources its information from the national, international and global stage. It also provides news about upcoming events within the confines of the ministry.
It seeks to keep members abreast with current happenings that can impact their lives thus pushing them to align to the demands of the times.

Members of this arm are being trained on how to dig and report truthful but informative news around them and also report these happenings with standards.

LOFAEX sees News as that fragment of communication that informs individuals of the latest events, concerns, and personages in the globe making impact and changing the course of things.
This arm therefore, seeks to furnish members of VOTNEG and non-members with information that enables them make appropriate decisions about their lives, denominations, families, societies, and states. Despite the fact that it is diverting and pleasurable in nature, it’s worth is found in its ability to equip VOTNEG members and partners with knowledge that puts them ahead of their peers.
Being informed, drives away the repercussions of ignorance!
